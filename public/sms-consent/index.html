Last updated: January 30, 2026

SMS Program Information

Program Name: OptiBay AI SMS Notifications

Operated By: OptiBay AI LLC

Contact: joe@optibayai.com

What Messages You May Receive

When you opt in to OptiBay AI SMS notifications, you may receive the following types of messages:

  • Appointment confirmations
  • Appointment reminders
  • Vehicle repair and service status updates
  • Scheduling changes or notifications
  • Two-way customer support messaging (you can reply to messages)

Important: No marketing or promotional messages will be sent unless you separately opt in to marketing communications.

How to Opt In

Opt-in is collected during the appointment booking or service intake workflow in OptiQueue (the OptiBay AI scheduling platform). Customers opt in by providing their mobile phone number and checking an SMS consent checkbox before submitting the form.

Important: The checkbox is unchecked by default and requires an affirmative user action to opt in.
Revocation: Consent can be revoked at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Consent Checkbox Language:

"I agree to receive SMS messages from OptiBay AI regarding my vehicle service appointments, including appointment confirmations, reminders, and status updates. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase."

Consent Terms

Consent is not a condition of purchase. You can complete booking or service intake without opting in to SMS notifications. Opting out will not affect your ability to receive service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your appointment activity. You will typically receive messages when:

  • An appointment is scheduled or confirmed
  • A reminder is sent before your appointment
  • Your vehicle's service status changes
  • You initiate a support conversation

Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

STOP / HELP

  • Reply STOP to cancel SMS notifications at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
  • Reply HELP for assistance or more information about the SMS program.
  • You can also contact support directly at joe@optibayai.com.
